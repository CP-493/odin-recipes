Macaroni Cheese

The Best Mac and Cheese

  • PREP TIME: 15 MINS
  • COOK TIME: 20 MINS
  • TOTAL TIME: 35 MINS

Ingredients

For the meat sauce

  • 340 grams chickpea pasta elbows, dry
  • 28 grams of butter
  • 12 grams of xanthan gum
  • 360 ml of unsweetened almond milk
  • 360 ml of chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 125 grams of shredded cheddar cheese

Method

  1. Prepare the chickpea macaroni according to the package instructions and set aside.
  2. Brown the ground beef
  3. In a large pot, melt the butter until it’s slightly bubbly. Add the xanthan gum and mix it with the butter well.
  4. Mix in the unsweetened almond milk, broth, and salt and simmer for 5–6 minutes, stirring regularly, until the sauce has thickened slightly.
  5. Stir in the shredded cheese until it melts.
  6. Add the cooked pasta and mix it well to evenly distribute the sauce
  7. SERVE AND ENJOY!
